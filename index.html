Hello!

My name is Abdul.


Born and raised in Orange County, CA.

I'm currently a third-year student studying Informatics (and soon to be specializing in Human Computer Interaction) at the University of California, Irvine.

 


First things first, I am a HUGE tech guy! Growing up, I've always been surrounded by electronics and videogames thanks to my older brother. My first console was a Sega Dreamcast (where I was pretty OP in Power Stone) and my first operating system was the good ol' Windows XP.


A good portion of my childhood and teenage years was spent playing MMO's like Runes of Magic (I couldn't afford the WoW subscription) or console classics like the Final Fantasy series. Nowadays though, I only play Overwatch or Smash Ultimate if I'm ever allotted the time.


I must admit, being a student while trying to balance outside work like creating your own portfolio is difficult if you lack the passion for it. Prior to web development, I was studying to become a backend developer as a computer science major but felt too restricted. I wanted to tap into both 'stem' and visual design so I made the switch to study Informatics.

I've never looked back since then because learning web development and visibly seeing the products, software, and other fruits of labor you make feels so rewarding!


As I develop into a professional, I intend to act upon the two words indicated on my logo - generative and resilient. I want to continuously produce meaningful, intentional content and carry the drive to progress as an individual and as a developer.

If I ever need to catch a break though, odds are you can probably find me doing a photoshoot somewhere or editing those pictures on Lightroom & Photoshop.
